Learn to nest Blueprints and the precedence of template folders and resource files.

It is composed of three levels of nested Blueprints:

  • admin page
    This page remains as it was when you registered it.
  • user page
    This page overwrites the template in the middle layer.
  • guest page
    This page overwrites the template once in the middle layer, and then overwrites it again in the upper layer.
